__NOTOC__

## General Notes

If you use any of these templates **inside a table** and they **mess it
up** somehow make sure you add a newline after the "\|".
e.g.:

    {|
    ...
    ...
    |previous entry
    |
    {{MW}}
    |next entry
    ...
    ...
    |}
