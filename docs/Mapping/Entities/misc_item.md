## General

This allows you to place items like weapons, armour or bombs into maps.

## Keys

- **item**: the item to spawn. This must be a valid
  [objdef](UFO-Scripts/weapon_*.ufo "wikilink") found in the
  [UFO-Scripts](UFO-Scripts "wikilink")
