## General

- This object can hurt an actor when he's on a field that is touched by
  this brush.
- Assign the texture

## Keys

- *Spawnflags*:
  - **KILL**: Decreases the HP of the actor
  - **STUN**: Increases the stun points of the actor
  - **DAZE**: Dazes the actor as soon as he gets in touch
